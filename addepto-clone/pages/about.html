Our Story

Founded in 2014 by a team of passionate data scientists and AI researchers, Addepto began as a small startup with a big vision: to democratize artificial intelligence for businesses across all industries.

Over the years, we've grown from a team of 5 to over 50 AI experts, serving clients ranging from innovative startups to Fortune 500 companies. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible with AI.
